What is A2 Milk?

A2 milk is pure dairy milk, completely natural and additive free, which is why it tastes so refreshing. Most cows milk today contains 2 main types of beta-casein protein, A2 and A1. Originally, India was totally a A2 producing country as our desi, native indigenous breeds all with a hump produces A2 milk. The imported breeds viz., HF, Jersey etc etc produce mainly A1 type . A1 milk is associated with diabetics, Cardio problems & other health related issues. We have chosen GIR native breed which is considered the best breed producing A2 milk.. switch to A2 milk.
